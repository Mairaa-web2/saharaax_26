About SahaaraX

SahaaraX is an AI-powered digital platform designed specifically for organizations working towards women's safety, empowerment, and recovery. Our system provides a centralized solution to manage key services including shelter details, therapy sessions, training progress, and session participation.

From shelter to education and mental healing, SahaaraX supports women at every stage of their recovery journey. The platform uses artificial intelligence to simplify tasks such as analyzing psychological test results, identifying improvement levels, and tracking progress.
